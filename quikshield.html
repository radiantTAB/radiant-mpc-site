Features

What QuikShield does.

The NCRP 151 worked-example methodology, turned into a guided workflow.

NCRP 151 Engine

Primary barriers, secondary leakage and scatter, neutron shielding at 10 MV and above, and maze/door calculations.

Drawing Calibration

Drop in a PDF or PNG plan view, set the scale with two clicks, then click the points of interest — distances and scatter angles compute automatically.

Step-by-Step Audit Trail

Every barrier shows its formula, the substituted values, the result, and the NCRP citation.

Clinic-Ready Reports

Generates a Word report with the annotated drawing embedded, ready for review and submission.

Editable Reference Data

All NCRP tabulated data lives in inspectable JSON files — adjust values without touching code.

Optional AI Sanity Check

Optional plain-language explanations and a review pass over inputs and results — every calculation works without it.

Who It's For

Made for vault shielding design.

QuikShield is built for clinical physicists and facility planners who design or evaluate megavoltage external-beam treatment vaults. It implements the NCRP Report 151 methodology — primary barriers, secondary leakage and scatter, neutron shielding, and maze/door — in a guided workflow.

The whole tool runs on your workstation; no project data leaves the machine unless you explicitly enable the optional AI features. Because every barrier carries a step-by-step audit trail with NCRP citations, the resulting report is defensible and ready to hand to a reviewer or surveyor.
