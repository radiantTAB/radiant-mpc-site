Services

What Radiant does.

Independent medical physics consulting for radiation oncology programs. Each engagement is scoped to a deliverable you can hand to a colleague, a surveyor, or a vendor.

ESAPI Scripting & Eclipse Automation

Custom C# plugins for the Eclipse Scripting API. Single-file scripts or compiled DLLs, deployed to your shared script directory and version-controlled. Examples of past work:

  • Physics chart-check hub with contour sanity check
  • Optimization objective analyzer with QUANTEC / TG-101 OAR rules
  • Portal dosimetry batch QA with PDF reporting into Aria
  • Auto-add planar fields, beam-naming validators, jaw-tracking checks

Eclipse 15.6 environment fully supported; compatible with multi-linac installations including TrueBeam and iX.

Portal Dosimetry & PSQA Commissioning

End-to-end commissioning of portal dosimetry for VMAT pre-treatment QA, including baseline image acquisition, DLG investigation, gamma analysis protocol setup, and ongoing constancy QA aligned with AAPM TG-307. Deliverable includes a commissioning manual suitable for surveyor review and SOP documentation for daily/weekly/monthly QA.

Plan QA, SBRT & Reirradiation Review

Independent plan reviews for complex cases — SBRT, SRS, reirradiation — with BED/EQD2 cumulative-dose tracking, OAR constraint analysis against published consensus (Timmerman, QUANTEC, TG-101), and structured findings reports. HDR brachytherapy chart checks and GYN dose-tracking workflows also supported.

Regulatory & Credentialing Support

Ohio QMP credentialing pathway navigation (OAC 3701:1-58 and 3701:1-67), AAPM MPPG 9.a/9.b SBRT supervision documentation, policy and procedure review, and provisional pathway guidance for programs adding new modalities.

Custom & Hourly Engagements

Not seeing your exact need above? Most consulting requests fall into hourly support — second-opinion plan reviews, ESAPI troubleshooting, vendor-correspondence review, or QA program audits. Get in touch to scope.
