QuikDose Web Calculator

EQD2 / BED dose accumulation.

Linear-quadratic radiobiology for HDR brachytherapy and external-beam re-irradiation — single-course conversion, multi-course cumulative summation, and remaining-budget back-conversion. Runs entirely in your browser.

Decision support only — not validated for clinical use. This calculator computes linear-quadratic EQD2/BED from the values you type; it does not read plan or dose data. Every result must be independently verified by a Qualified Medical Physicist before any clinical use. Built-in α/β values are starting points — your clinic's policy takes precedence. Do not enter PHI; nothing is saved or transmitted.

Method

Linear-quadratic model, with total dose D delivered in n fractions of d = D / n:

EQD2 = D × (d + α/β) ÷ (2 + α/β)

BED = D × (1 + d ÷ α/β)

Back-conversion of a remaining EQD2 budget over n remaining fractions, with e = budget ÷ n:

d = [ −(α/β) + √( (α/β)² + 4·e·(2 + α/β) ) ] ÷ 2

The same equations used by the QuikDose Eclipse plugins. This calculator does no spatial/voxel summation — for voxel-level cumulative EQD2 across dose grids, use the QuikDose EBRT binary plugin.
