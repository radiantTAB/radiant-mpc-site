Radiant QuikQA is PyLinac-powered machine QA for radiation oncology physics — Winston-Lutz, Picket Fence, VMAT, TG-142 leaf-speed, and TG-51 dosimetry, all in one standalone Windows application. Winston-Lutz handles MV, kV, and CBCT isocenter and BB-coincidence with sub-millimeter reporting. Picket Fence checks MLC leaf positional accuracy for your TG-142 monthly tests. VMAT QA analyzes dose-rate and MLC-speed constancy from a single delivery. The TG-142 leaf-speed module works straight from machine logs — per-leaf RMS error and beam holds, with no portal dosimetry required. TG-51 dosimetry covers photon and electron reference calibration with full chamber and electrometer catalogs. A built-in DICOM anonymizer lets you share test data safely, and the PDF toolkit finishes your reports. It all runs from a single executable — no install, no Python. Radiant QuikQA — precision physics, in software.
