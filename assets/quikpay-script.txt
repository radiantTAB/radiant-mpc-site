RADIANT QUIKPAY — DEMO NARRATION SCRIPT
Approximately 60 seconds. Paste into a voice-over tool, or record in your own voice.

Radiant QuikPay is one hundred percent local payroll for the small consulting LLC — no cloud, no subscription. It opens on a dashboard with year-to-date payroll, tax, and owner-draw totals at a glance. Add W-2 employees with full federal and Ohio withholding fields; Social Security numbers are encrypted at rest. Running payroll is a single screen — QuikPay calculates gross pay, federal and Ohio withholding, and FICA, then you preview and post. Every run produces a printable pay stub, with this-period and year-to-date columns. The ledger keeps every pay run with running totals, and exports to CSV. Employer taxes — FICA match, FUTA, and Ohio SUTA — are tracked for Forms 941 and 940, and year-end W-2 figures export straight to your CPA. Owner draws are logged separately from payroll — not wages, no withholding. Radiant QuikPay — payroll that stays on your machine.
