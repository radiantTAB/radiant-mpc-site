About

Built by a physicist,
for physicists.

Radiant Medical Physics Consulting is the consulting practice of Todd Bernath, MS — an Ohio-based medical physicist with two decades of clinical experience in external-beam radiation therapy, brachytherapy, and the software that runs them.

Background

Todd's primary clinical practice spans Dayton Physicians Network and Kettering Health, where he serves as a treatment-planning and QA physicist supporting TrueBeam and iX linacs, HDR brachytherapy programs, and SBRT/SRS commissioning. He founded Radiant Medical Physics Consulting in 2024 to formalize the consulting work he had been doing on the side — Eclipse scripting, second-opinion plan reviews, and regulatory support for smaller programs.

Why Radiant
